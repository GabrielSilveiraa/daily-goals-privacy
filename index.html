Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2025

TL;DR: Daily Goals does NOT collect, store, or share ANY of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

Introduction

Welcome to Daily Goals. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains our approach to data privacy, which is simple: we don't collect any of your data.

Data Collection

Daily Goals does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or information. All your habit data, including:

  • Your habits and goals
  • Completion records and streaks
  • Statistics and progress data
  • App settings and preferences
  • Any other information you enter in the app

...is stored locally on your device only and never leaves your device.

Data Storage

All data is stored using iOS's standard local storage (UserDefaults and App Groups). This means:

  • No cloud synchronization: Your data is not synced to iCloud or any other cloud service
  • No server communication: The app does not connect to any servers or external services
  • No analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking services
  • No third-party services: The app does not integrate with any third-party services
  • No advertisements: The app does not display ads or use advertising networks

Your Data Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to:

  • Request access to
  • Request deletion of
  • Request export of
  • Request modification of
  • Share with third parties

You own all your data, and it never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all your data is deleted from your device.

Widget Data

Daily Goals includes home screen widgets that display your habit progress. This data is shared between the main app and the widget extension using iOS App Groups, but remains entirely on your device. No data is transmitted externally.

Children's Privacy

Daily Goals does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your privacy.

Third-Party Links

This app does not contain any links to external websites or services. All functionality is self-contained within the app.

Data Security

Your data is as secure as your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS device updated with the latest security patches
  • Using device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID protection
  • Not jailbreaking your device

International Data Transfers

Since no data leaves your device, there are no international data transfers to disclose.
